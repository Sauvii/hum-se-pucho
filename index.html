Jageshwar Temple

Jageshwar Temples, also referred to as Jageswar Temples or Jageshwar Valley Temples, are a group of 125 ancient Hindu temples dated between 7th and 14th century, near Almora in Almora district of the Himalayan ndian state of Uttarakhand. The valley has a number of temple clusters such as the Dandeshwar and ageshwar sites.
